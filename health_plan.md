# Health Plan — Ryan Desmond

## Goal
275 lbs to 225 lbs by September 3, 2026 (49th birthday).
Rate: ~2.3 lbs/week.

## Daily Targets
- Calories: 1,900 cal/day
- Protein: 205-225g/day
- Water: 100+ oz

## Meal Plan

### Breakfast (~450 cal, ~45g protein)
- 3 eggs scrambled or hard-boiled
- 1/2 cup rolled oats with 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Coffee (black or with splash of almond milk)

### Lunch (~500 cal, ~55g protein)
- 6 oz grilled chicken (thigh or breast)
- Large salad (sunflower salad bag) with olive oil dressing
- Side of beans or lentils (1/2 cup)

### Dinner (~550 cal, ~60g protein)
- 6 oz protein (chicken, fish, or lean meat)
- Roasted vegetables or steamed broccoli
- 1/2 cup rice or sweet potato

### Snacks (~400 cal, ~50g protein)
- Protein shake (1 scoop protein powder + almond milk)
- Greek yogurt (plain, 1 cup)
- Hard-boiled eggs (2)
- Banana

## Weekly Workout Schedule

- **Monday**: Strength A — Push + Legs
- **Tuesday**: Away — Treadmill + Bodyweight
- **Wednesday**: Strength B — Pull + Hinge
- **Thursday**: Row or Bike (cardio, 30 min)
- **Friday**: Rest
- **Saturday**: Strength C — Full Body
- **Sunday**: Yoga / Walk (recovery, 30 min)

## Sunday Prep (~30 min active)
1. Make chili (~45 min, mostly passive) — portion into 7 containers, freeze 2
2. Hard boil 10 eggs (~12 min) — grab-and-go snacks all week
3. Freeze bananas (2 min) — peel, bag, freeze for smoothies
